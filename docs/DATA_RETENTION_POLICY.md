# MED DROP Data Retention Policy

## Overview

MED DROP maintains comprehensive records of all medical courier operations for compliance and operational purposes.

## Retention Period

**All records are retained for 7 years from the date of completion/delivery.**

This includes:
- Load requests and tracking events
- Proof of delivery documents (POD)
- Digital signatures (pickup and delivery)
- Temperature logs
- Chain-of-custody records
- Invoices and billing records
- Driver attestations
- All related documentation

## Data Types Retained

### Load Records
- Load request details
- Status history and tracking events
- Route information
- Service specifications

### Compliance Records
- Digital signatures (pickup and delivery)
- Proof of delivery documents
- Chain-of-custody logs (who had custody, when, where)
- Driver attestations
- Temperature readings and exception logs

### Financial Records
- Invoices
- Payment records
- Quote history
- Billing correspondence

### Operational Records
- Driver assignments
- Facility information
- Shipper information
- Document uploads

## Data Export

All data is exportable upon request. To request data export:

1. Submit a request to support@meddrop.com
2. Include the load tracking code(s) or date range
3. Specify the format needed (PDF, CSV, JSON)
4. Allow 5-7 business days for processing

## Data Deletion

After the 7-year retention period:
- Records may be archived to cold storage
- Records may be deleted per legal requirements
- Financial records may be retained longer per tax/accounting requirements

## HIPAA & Healthcare Compliance

While MED DROP does not store protected health information (PHI) in most cases, we maintain:
- Access controls on all records
- Audit logs for document access
- Secure storage of all compliance documents

## Contact

For questions about data retention or to request data export:
- Email: support@meddrop.com
- Include "Data Retention Request" in subject line
